# Utilization - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the three parts of each utilization pattern (Observe, Normalize, Lead).

1. "I see you're looking around... that's fine... and as you look around, you can let your eyes land on whatever feels comfortable."
2. "You say you're feeling anxious... and that's okay... and that anxiety can actually help you focus more deeply."
3. "I notice you're quiet... that's perfect... and in that quiet, you can hear your inner wisdom."
4. "You're tapping your foot... that's natural... and that rhythm can help you relax."
5. "I see you're skeptical... and that's healthy... and skepticism can help you find what truly works."

**Answers**:
1. Observe: looking around | Normalize: fine | Lead: let eyes land on comfortable spot
2. Observe: feeling anxious | Normalize: okay | Lead: anxiety helps focus
3. Observe: quiet | Normalize: perfect | Lead: hear inner wisdom
4. Observe: tapping foot | Normalize: natural | Lead: rhythm helps relax
5. Observe: skeptical | Normalize: healthy | Lead: skepticism helps find what works
