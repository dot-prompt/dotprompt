## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the unspecified verb in each sentence and what process it leaves open.

1. "You can learn quickly."
2. "Things will get better."

4. "You'll change in the right way."
5. "Growth happens naturally."

4. "change" - how is undefined
5. "happens" - cause/process not specified
