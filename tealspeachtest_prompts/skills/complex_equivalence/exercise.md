**Instructions**: Identify the complex equivalence pattern in the following sentences. What is the observation? What is the implied meaning?

1. "Your pause means you're considering carefully."
2. "The fact that you're here shows your commitment."
3. "Your question indicates you're ready for more."
4. "Your smile tells me you're on the right track."

**Answers**:
1. Observation: pause, Implied meaning: considering carefully
2. Observation: being here, Implied meaning: commitment
3. Observation: question, Implied meaning: readiness for more
4. Observation: smile, Implied meaning: being on right track
