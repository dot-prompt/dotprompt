# Punctuation Ambiguity - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the punctuation ambiguity pattern (run-on sentences with bridge words) in the following sentences.

1. "Your unconscious mind knows and knows how to help you."
2. "You're learning easily and easily going deeper."
3. "I don't know how quickly understanding comes."
4. "You can relax and relaxation deepens."
5. "The change is happening and happening now."

**Answers**:
1. Bridge word "knows" connects two sentences
2. Bridge word "easily" creates continuous flow
3. Bridge word "how" runs sentences together
4. Bridge word "and" connects sentences
5. Bridge word "and" + "happening" repeated
