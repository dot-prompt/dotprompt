# Clinical Practice - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify which NLP components are being demonstrated in the following clinical statements.

1. "I notice you're sitting here today, and as you do, you might become aware of how ready you are for this change."

2. "You came in with a concern, and while we're exploring it, let's also notice what strengths you already have."

3. "As you describe that experience, you might begin to see it from a different perspective."

**Answers**:
1. Pacing (acknowledging current reality), Leading (inviting awareness), Reframing (readiness for change)
2. Pacing (acknowledging concern), Reframing (shifting to strengths)
3. Pacing (describing experience), Leading (beginning to see differently), Reframing (different perspective)
