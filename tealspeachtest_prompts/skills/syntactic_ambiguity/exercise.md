# Syntactic Ambiguity - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the syntactic ambiguity (multiple grammatical parses) in each sentence.

1. "I saw the man with the telescope."
2. "Flying planes can be dangerous."
3. "They are visiting relatives."
4. "The horse raced past the barn fell."
5. "Hypnotizing hypnotists can be tricky."

**Answers**:
1. With the telescope modifies "man" (he had it) or is instrument (I used it)
2. Planes that are flying are dangerous or act of flying planes is dangerous
3. Visiting them or being visited by them
4. Raced is past tense or participial adjective
5. Tricky to hypnotize hypnotists or hypnotists hypnotizing people
