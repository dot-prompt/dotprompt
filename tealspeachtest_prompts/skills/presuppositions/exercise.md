# Presuppositions - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the presupposition (assumed truth) in each sentence.

1. "As you continue reading, you'll discover your own wisdom."
2. "The moment you relax, the change becomes permanent."
3. "Before you realize it, you'll have already changed."
4. "Your ability to learn grows stronger every day."
5. "While you're thinking about this, the solution emerges."

**Answers**:
1. Presupposition: You will discover your own wisdom (assumed)
2. Presupposition: Relaxing will happen; change will be permanent
3. Presupposition: Change will happen (it's inevitable)
4. Presupposition: You have ability to learn (it exists and grows)
5. Presupposition: A solution exists and will emerge
