# Conversational Postulates - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the conversational postulate in each sentence. What is the implied action?

1. "Would you like to make yourself comfortable?"
2. "Could you notice your breathing?"
3. "Might you be willing to try something new?"

**Answers**:
1. Sitting down / becoming comfortable
2. Noticing breathing
3. Trying something new
