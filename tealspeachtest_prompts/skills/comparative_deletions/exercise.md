# Comparative Deletions - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the comparative deletion pattern in the following sentences. What is being compared? What is deleted?

1. "It's better to relax now."
2. "You can go deeper into this state."
3. "Learning becomes easier with practice."
4. "You're becoming more confident."
5. "The sooner you start, the better."

**Answers**:
1. "better" - deleted: better than what? (listener decides)
2. "deeper" - deleted: deeper than what?
3. "easier" - deleted: easier than what?
4. "more confident" - deleted: more confident than what?
5. "the sooner the better" - deleted: better than what?
