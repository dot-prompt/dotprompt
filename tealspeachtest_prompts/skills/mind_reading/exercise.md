# Mind Reading - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the mind reading pattern (statements about listener's internal state) in each sentence.

1. "I know you're feeling excited about this."
2. "You must be wondering what comes next."
3. "I can see you're thinking deeply about this."
4. "You probably feel a bit uncertain, and that's okay."
5. "I understand that this makes sense to you."

**Answers**:
1. Claims to know listener's feeling (excitement)
2. Claims to know listener's wondering
3. Claims to see listener's thinking
4. Claims to know listener's emotional state (uncertainty)
5. Claims to understand listener's comprehension
