# Scope Ambiguity - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the scope ambiguity pattern (unclear modifier boundaries) in the following sentences.

1. "The old men and women."
2. "Your positive beliefs and attitudes will grow."
3. "I spoke to the concerned manager and staff."
4. "The disturbing thoughts and feelings can release."
5. "Your happy memories and hopes emerge."

**Answers**:
1. "old" could apply to both "men and women" or just "men"
2. "positive" could apply to both or just "beliefs"
3. "concerned" could apply to both or just "manager"
4. "disturbing" could apply to both or just "thoughts"
5. "happy" could apply to both or just "memories"
