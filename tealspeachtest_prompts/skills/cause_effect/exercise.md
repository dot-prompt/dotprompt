# Cause Effect - Practice Exercises

## Exercise 1: Recognition

**Instructions**: Identify the cause-effect pattern in each sentence.

1. "Because you're listening, you can learn."
2. "As you consider this, confidence grows."
3. "When you relax, change happens naturally."
4. "By paying attention, you discover your own wisdom."
5. "The more you practice, the better you become."

**Answers**:
1. Listening causes learning
2. Considering causes confidence growth
3. Relaxing causes change
4. Paying attention causes wisdom discovery
5. Practice causes improvement
